About CarrerCraft

Who we are

India's most practical, result-driven pharmaceutical career training and consultancy agency — built by practitioners, not academics.

CarrerCraft was founded on a single belief: every pharmacy graduate deserves expert guidance to find their best career path in India's booming pharmaceutical industry.

India produces over 1.5 lakh pharmacy graduates annually. Yet most enter the workforce without a clear roadmap — unaware of 24+ career paths, unaware of salary benchmarks, and unprepared for MNC pharma interviews.

CarrerCraft changes that. We bring the industry to the training room — with working pharma professionals as trainers, real-world simulations as practice, and end-to-end placement support as our commitment.

"We don't train you with theory. We train you with the exact knowledge, language and skills that pharma companies look for — because our trainers are sitting inside those companies right now."

Our Services

6 core services — from confusion to career success

Each service is designed to take a pharmacy graduate from where they are to where they want to be.

1
Pharmaceutical Career Domain Training

Intensive expert-led modules across Sales & Marketing, QA/QC, Clinical Research, Regulatory Affairs, Pharmacovigilance, Hospital Pharmacy, Production, R&D, Medical Coding and Pharma Digital Marketing. Each module built around real job descriptions and MNC hiring standards.

Sales & MR Training QA/QC Certification Clinical Research Regulatory Affairs Pharmacovigilance Medical Coding
2
Mock Interview & Placement Preparation

Structured mock interviews modelled on how Abbott, Cipla, Sun Pharma, Novo Nordisk and GSK actually hire. Includes product detailing role-plays, technical rounds, HR rounds, group discussions and panel sessions with real working professionals.

Product Detailing Simulations Technical Interview Prep HR Round Coaching GD Practice
3
CV Building & LinkedIn Optimization

Pharma-specific resume crafting that makes recruiters stop scrolling. Industry-standard CV formats, keyword optimization for pharma job portals, achievement-based language, and a LinkedIn profile that attracts inbound recruiter messages.

ATS-Optimized CV LinkedIn Profile Makeover Job Portal Strategy
4
Career Counselling & Domain Selection

One-on-one sessions to map your academic strengths, personal interests and market demand to the right pharma domain. Using our SOAR framework to identify your ideal career pathway — whether it is Sales, QA/QC, Clinical Research, Government jobs or Higher Studies.

Free First Session Subject-to-Career Mapping Salary Benchmarking
5
D.Pharm to B.Pharm Lateral Entry Guidance

Complete roadmap for D.Pharm graduates to upgrade qualifications through lateral entry into B.Pharm 2nd year — unlocking all 24 pharma career pathways. Covers eligibility, top colleges, admission process and career comparison between D.Pharm-only and B.Pharm routes.

Lateral Entry Process College Selection D.Pharm vs B.Pharm Guide
6
Higher Studies Advisory — GPAT, MBA & MS Abroad

Expert guidance on M.Pharm via GPAT, MBA in Pharma or Healthcare Management through CAT/MAT/GMAT, and MS/PhD in Pharmaceutical Sciences in USA, UK, Canada and Australia. Includes exam strategy, college shortlisting, SOP review and scholarship guidance.

GPAT Preparation MBA Pharma Counselling MS Abroad Guidance Scholarship Advisory
